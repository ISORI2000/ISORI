We’re a diverse group of 10 Grade 9 students who wish to make a beneficial change,
through volunteering. Our team aims to provide more accessible volunteering, and
recruiting opportunities. Our platform, ISori, was created to align with these
ambitions. ISori (아이소리), which can be translated into ‘children sound’ in Korean,
embodies how students like us can ‘make sound’ and spread awareness.
